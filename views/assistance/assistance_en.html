  • 1.What is Inschain mutual aid platform?

  • 2.What is the reasons for set up a mutual aid platform?

    In daily life , accidents, health, account security, family responsibilities, etc., all kinds of risks seem to be far away from us. In fact, such accidents have frequently occurred around us. We have witnessed the huge impact of these incidents on families again and again. We have been thinking about whether there is a third model beyond commercial insurance and public welfare – both to provide risk protection and care for others? We researched deeply into the origin of insurance—mutual aid, and demonstrated the mutual aid model based on blockchain technology will be strongly benefits members and shine again. So, we created "Inschain mutual aid project" ,which perfectly combines protection and care and truly realizes the relationship between contribution and deserve; love and care.

  • 15.What are the reasons for listing the exclusion of protection? How to verify these situations?

    The essence of Inschain mutual aid is the mutual commitment promised by all mutual aid members for uncertain future risks. The premise of achieving fairness is that the probability that mutual aid members face potential risks is about the same. If people suffering from cancer, major diseases, high-risk occupations are equally protected, the rules are unfair to other members.
    The Inschain mutual aid platform cannot verify the physical condition of the members beforehand. Therefore, the platform requires members to truthfully inform the plan before joining the plan and set up reasonable cases of exclusion. After the incident occurs, the platform must confirm that the incident actually happened and exclude the situation mentions in the product rules before initiating mutual assistance.

  • 17.How to prevent members from defrauding mutual aid payments?

    A mutual aid member may voluntarily apply for a withdrawal plan from the Mutual Guarantee Platform. However, after exiting, you do not have the right to request the Mutual Assistance Platform or other members to initiate mutual assistance for you in the event of a mutual aid event in the future.
    The reason for the introduction of independent third-party assessment agencies and expert committees in the Inschain Mutual aid Platform is to maximize the veracity of mutual aid incidents. The assessment agency will have a complete and rigorous survey plan, and experts can judge the incident from their professional perspective. We will make announcements about each stage in the mutual find distribution process, so that each member can play a supervisory role. In the event that fraudulent activity is discovered after the mutual fund transfer, the platform will take the obligation to recover the money.
